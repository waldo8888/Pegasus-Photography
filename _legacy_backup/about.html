About Pegasus

For over 15 years, we've been capturing the moments that matter most— helping families and schools preserve precious memories through beautiful photography.

Our Story

Built on Passion, Driven by Purpose

Pegasus School Images was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to create beautiful, authentic photographs that families would treasure for generations.

What started as a small studio has grown into a trusted partner for over 200 schools across the region. But our commitment to quality and personal service has never changed.

We believe every portrait should tell a story—whether it's capturing a senior's excitement for the future or documenting the everyday magic of a school community.
