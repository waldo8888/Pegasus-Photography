The Process

Your Graduation Experience

From booking to framing, we make your graduation portrait experience seamless and memorable.

01

The Session

Your 15-20 minute sitting includes traditional poses in your gown and hood, plus casual shots to show your personality. We provide the regalia for your school.

02

The Proofs

No need to wait. Your proofs are emailed directly to you shortly after your session. View them on your phone or laptop and share with family.

03

The Composite

A tradition that lasts. Your portrait will be included in the official class composite board that hangs in your department hallway.

Details

What to Expect

Sitting Fee

A $25.00 sitting fee (+ tax) is required to book your appointment. This covers the photographer's time and equipment.

*Note: This fee does not include prints or digital files.

Wardrobe

We provide the gown, sash, and hood for your specific degree. For the casual portion, wear business casual or something that represents you.

Double Degrees

Graduating with two degrees? No problem. We can photograph you in both hoods during your session. Just let the photographer know.
